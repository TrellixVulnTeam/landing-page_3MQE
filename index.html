    Uber, Guangzhou China
    At Uber China, I worked in Product Operations. Specifically, my team was tasked with tracking local rideshare competitors DiDi ChuXing (滴滴出行), ShenZhou ZhuanChe (神州专车), and Yidao YongChe (一道用车). While full-time programmers and I created web applications that handled data, the rest of the team used them to analyze the competition. They handled user input to perform key calculations, visualizations, and other means of analysis over the continually updating databases. I utilized HTML5/CSS3, jQuery, Javascript, Java, PHP, SQL, and Linux shell script. Co-workers and superiors used these tools to better understand competitors, marketshare, and rivals' driver subsidy strategies.
